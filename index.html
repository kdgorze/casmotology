About Me

Passion behind every move.

Hi! I'm Casmon, an aspiring cosmetologist with a deep love for discovering one's inner and outer beauty. I am a current student in cosmetology school and I'm ready to bring my creativity, precision, and care to every new client.

Whether you're looking for a bold new color, a fresh cut, or a gorgeous updo for a special event, I'm here to make your vision a reality. My adaptablitiy and prefectionism makes sure each new client will get exactly what they are looking for.

I believe great looks starts with great communication. I will always take the time to listen to a client's wants, while giving my own honest feedback about what I think personally suits them best.
